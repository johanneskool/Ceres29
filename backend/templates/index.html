When you click "Choose a file", you can upload your own, new file, or choose a file that is already used before. After uploading a new file, go the this menu again, and choose your file from the on the right. After loading the data, you can choose from different types of visualisations to compare the network in multiple ways. When clicking a node in one of the node-link diagrams, you will be able to see details about the node and the edges to and from that node. When clicking a cell in the adjacency matrix, you will see the details about the current relation.
