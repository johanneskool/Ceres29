Welcome at the Ceres29 tool for network visualisation! This is how to use the tool:
1. When you click "Choose a file" in the menu above, you can upload your own, new file, or choose a file that is already used before.
2. After choosing a file, select one of the four different visualisation types and one of the six ways to order your data.
3. Your visualisation will be shown on the next page. It includes multiple interactions like zooming, selecting nodes or edges, searching a specific node, dragging nodes, and multiple other interactions.
4. Have fun exploring your data and getting new insights with our tool!
